Strange Tacos

Description

Tacos coul be differrent and it depends of your country, so I give you a recipe for my own coountry

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup beef or chicken broth
  • Hard or soft taco shells
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, etc.)
  • Diced avocado or guacamole
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Sour cream or salsa (optional, for topping)

Steps

  1. Cook the beef filling:
    • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they become translucent, then add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
    • Add ground beef to the skillet and cook, breaking it apart with a spoon, until browned and cooked through.
    • Drain excess fat from the skillet if necessary. Add chili powder, ground cumin, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine
    • Pour in the beef or chicken broth and let the mixture simmer for 5-7 minutes until the liquid reduces and the flavors meld together. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  2. Preapare taco shells and toppings:
    • If using hard taco shells, warm them according to the package instructions. If using soft tortillas, warm them in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable.
    • Prepare the toppings: shred lettuce, dice tomatoes, shred cheese, chop cilantro, and prepare any other desired toppings.
  3. Assemble the tacos:
    • Spoon the cooked beef filling into the warmed taco shells or tortillas.
    • Top the beef filling with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, diced avocado or guacamole, chopped cilantro, and any other toppings of your choice.
  4. Serve: Serve the assembled tacos immediately with optional toppings like sour cream or salsa on the side.
